Car Rental Policy

1. Reservations and Payment

  • Reservations can be made online or in person at our rental locations.
  • A valid driver's license and credit card are required to make a reservation.
  • Full payment is due at the time of vehicle pick-up.
  • We accept major credit cards and debit cards.

2. Age Requirements

  • The minimum age to rent a car is 21 years old.
  • Drivers under 25 may be subject to a young driver surcharge.

3. Driver's License and Insurance

  • All drivers must present a valid driver's license held for at least one year.
  • International drivers must present a valid domestic license, international driving permit, and passport.
  • We offer optional insurance coverage. Renters are responsible for any damage to the vehicle if insurance is declined.

4. Vehicle Pick-up and Return

  • Vehicles must be picked up and returned at the agreed-upon times. Late returns may incur additional charges.
  • Vehicles should be returned with the same level of fuel as at pick-up. A refueling fee will be charged if the tank is not filled to the appropriate level.

5. Cancellations and Modifications

  • Free cancellation is available up to 48 hours before the rental start time.
  • Cancellations made within 48 hours of the rental start time may be subject to a cancellation fee.
  • Modifications to reservations can be made subject to vehicle availability.

6. Prohibited Uses

  • Smoking is not permitted in any of our vehicles. A cleaning fee will be charged for violations.
  • Vehicles may not be taken off-road or used for racing or other prohibited activities.
  • Pets are only allowed in designated pet-friendly vehicles and may incur an additional cleaning fee.

7. Breakdowns and Accidents

  • In case of a breakdown or accident, contact our 24/7 roadside assistance immediately.
  • All accidents must be reported to the police and our rental office as soon as possible.

By making a reservation, you agree to abide by these policies. We reserve the right to refuse service to anyone who violates these terms.
